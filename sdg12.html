Goal 12: Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.

SDG 12 aims to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns, which is key to sustain the livelihoods of current and future generations. It promotes the efficient management of our shared natural resources, and encourages industries, businesses, and consumers to recycle and reduce waste.

Sustainable consumption and production can help mitigate the environmental impact of economic growth and ensure sustainable livelihoods for all.

Key Targets:

  • Implement the 10-Year Framework of Programmes on sustainable consumption and production.
  • Encourage companies to adopt sustainable practices.
  • Reduce food waste at the retail and consumer levels.

Why It Matters:

Sustainable consumption and production is essential for sustainable development and helps ensure that the use of resources and the impacts on the environment are minimized while promoting economic growth.
