Goal 3: Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages.

SDG 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all people, including significant reductions in maternal and child mortality, combating epidemics, and achieving universal health coverage.

Key Targets:

  • Reduce global maternal mortality to less than 70 per 100,000 live births.
  • End the epidemics of AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria, and neglected tropical diseases.
  • Ensure universal access to sexual and reproductive health care services.
  • Substantially increase health financing and recruitment, development, training, and retention of the health workforce.

Why It Matters:

Health is a key component of sustainable development. By ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being, we can improve productivity and quality of life for everyone.
