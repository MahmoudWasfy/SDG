Goal 13: Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

SDG 13 calls for urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. It emphasizes the need for increased awareness and action on climate change at all levels, encouraging countries to integrate climate change measures into their policies and strategies.

Climate change is a pressing issue affecting every country and community, leading to increased natural disasters, rising sea levels, and adverse impacts on health and livelihoods.

Key Targets:

  • Strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards.
  • Integrate climate change measures into national policies, strategies, and planning.
  • Improve education, awareness, and human and institutional capacity on climate change mitigation, adaptation, and impact reduction.

Why It Matters:

Addressing climate change is essential for the survival of our planet. Without immediate action, the impacts of climate change will worsen, affecting food security, health, and overall well-being globally.
