Goal 2: End hunger, achieve food security, and promote sustainable agriculture.

SDG 2 aims to end hunger and ensure that everyone has access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food year-round by 2030. This goal emphasizes the importance of sustainable agriculture, which includes enhancing the productivity and incomes of small-scale farmers and ensuring equitable access to land, technology, and markets.

Key Targets:

  • End all forms of hunger and malnutrition.
  • Ensure all people have sufficient and nutritious food year-round.
  • Promote sustainable agricultural practices.
  • Maintain the genetic diversity of seeds, cultivated plants, and farmed animals.

Why It Matters:

Hunger and malnutrition have profound effects on individuals and communities, leading to health issues, lack of education, and social instability. By promoting food security and sustainable agriculture, we can contribute to the well-being of communities and the planet.
