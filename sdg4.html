Goal 4: Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all.

SDG 4 aims to ensure that all young people and a substantial proportion of adults, both men and women, achieve literacy and numeracy. It emphasizes the importance of free and quality primary and secondary education for all children.

Key Targets:

  • Ensure that all youth and a substantial proportion of adults achieve literacy and numeracy.
  • Ensure equal access for all women and men to affordable and quality technical, vocational, and tertiary education.
  • Build and upgrade education facilities that are child, disability, and gender sensitive.
  • Substantially increase the number of scholarships available to developing countries.

Why It Matters:

Education is a powerful tool for reducing inequality and improving economic growth. By ensuring access to quality education, we empower individuals and communities to break the cycle of poverty.
