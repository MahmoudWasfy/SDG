Goal 8: Promote sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment and decent work for all.

SDG 8 aims to promote inclusive and sustainable economic growth, employment, and decent work for all. This goal emphasizes the importance of supporting economic growth by achieving higher levels of productivity and ensuring equal access to opportunities.

The goal also highlights the need to improve global resource efficiency and promote development-oriented policies that encourage sustainable practices. Achieving SDG 8 is crucial for fostering economic resilience and reducing inequality in and among countries.

Key Targets:

  • Achieve higher levels of economic productivity through diversification, technological upgrading, and innovation.
  • Promote development-oriented policies that encourage entrepreneurship and job creation.
  • Improve access to financial services for small and medium-sized enterprises.
  • Protect labor rights and promote safe and secure working environments for all workers.

Why It Matters:

Sustainable economic growth is vital for improving living standards, reducing poverty, and addressing inequality. By promoting decent work opportunities and fostering economic resilience, we can create inclusive societies that benefit everyone.
