Goal 7: Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all.

SDG 7 aims to ensure universal access to affordable, reliable, and modern energy services by 2030. This goal emphasizes the importance of increasing the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix and promoting energy efficiency.

Sustainable energy solutions are crucial for economic development, environmental sustainability, and social equity. Achieving this goal involves enhancing international cooperation to facilitate access to clean energy research and technology, and promoting investment in energy infrastructure and clean energy technology.

Key Targets:

  • Ensure universal access to affordable electricity.
  • Increase the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix.
  • Double the rate of improvement in energy efficiency.
  • Promote sustainable energy technologies and innovation.

Why It Matters:

Energy is a key driver of development. Access to reliable and affordable energy can help to reduce poverty, improve health outcomes, and support economic growth. Transitioning to sustainable energy sources also contributes to combating climate change and protecting the environment.
